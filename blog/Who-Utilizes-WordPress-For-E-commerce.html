The past few years have revealed that many brick-and-mortar shops have attempted to establish an online presence. The potential of e-commerce is greater than it ever has. However, it can be difficult to navigate various platforms to select the most effective one. Why choose WordPress as an eCommerce platform?

The WordPress Content Management System (CMS) has numerous functions. It is most well-known as a platform for blogging. However, its flexibility and customization make it a good choice for virtually any type of website.

Being an eCommerce platform, WordPress offers much in the way of integrations and options for customization. There are a myriad of themes and plug-ins that you can utilize to add eCommerce features to your website, as the platform's accessibility of use and its ability to scale make it an ideal choice for both small and large.

Who uses WordPress to sell products?

There are some business types that are most suited to WordPress eCommerce website. These include stores that provide physical or digital goods or services or subscriptions.

However, small, medium and large can benefit from the variety of eCommerce features accessible to WordPress users. To access this feature, you must integrate WordPress with one or more other tools.

WordPress eCommerce Integrations

WordPress is able to connect with multiple eCommerce platforms and plug-ins. Integrating aspects of eCommerce (like shopping carts) into your existing blog is also possible.

You can also choose from a variety of integrations to meet your business' needs. The best choice will differ according to the size of your company, type of product, and general goals. Still, there's sure to be a suitable solution for you. Let's look at some of the most popular platforms right now.

WordPress and WooCommerce

WooCommerce is among the most well-known WordPress eCommerce integrations, and there are numerous reasons for its popularity. First, it offers an extensive selection of customizing options and add-ons. It can be added to your WordPress website in minutes, and establishing your site is easy with its installation wizard.

Plug-ins for WordPress e-commerce

WordPress plug-ins are complicated parts of code that may be incorporated into your site in only a couple of clicks. They don't require you to code functions on your own. Create online shops, add virtual shopping carts, and link payments to items sold with plug-ins. These sophisticated functions can seamlessly integrate into your WordPress webshop.

The primary functions that the WordPress E-commerce plug-ins can add to your site include:

  • Modification of the pages for products
  • payment gateways,
  • Additional capabilities for shopping carts, as well as checkouts,
  • Integration of shipping companies with online stores,
  • products and search engines,
  • Navigation improvements,
  • Integration with tax software
